Dealing with Reality: Accuracy, Errors, and How to Fix Them

Let‚Äôs be real. Both models can be wrong. They hallucinate, they carry biases from their training data, and they can misinterpret you. For any professional use, you have to understand these challenges and have strategies to mitigate them.
